About me

Hi! I'm Sahib Dhanjal and am currently in my first year studying towards a Masters in Robotics and Automation at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. My primary interest lies in mobile robotics, specifically mapping, localization and autonomous exploration! I also like working on pet projects, exploring and experimenting new tools to build efficient software. Besides engaging myself in side projects, hanging out with friends and studying, I'm a huge fan of watching and playing football (I mean soccer, haha!). I have been following Real Madrid ever since I was 17 years of age and have hardly missed watching any of their games since. If you want to know more about me, what I'm upto or just feeling social - feel free to get in touch, I'd love to hear from you!
