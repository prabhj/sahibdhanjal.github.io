About me

Hey there, I am Sahib Dhanjal, currently a graduate Robotics student at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. I received my bachelors in Mechanical Engineering in 2016 from BITS Pilani, India, after which I worked as a market facing resource for ExxonMobil Chemicals India for just over an year. My primary interest lies in mobile robotics, specifically localization, mapping and autonmous exploration. However, I periodically like to experiment with machine learning, computer vision and industrial applications of robotics as well!
